# Stay Safe Online – UPI Safety Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown payment requests  
- Always verify the sender before making payments  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Use official banking apps only  
- Enable two-factor authentication where possible  
- Regularly check your bank statements  
- Be cautious of calls claiming to be from bank officials  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes for receiving money  
- Report fraud immediately on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
