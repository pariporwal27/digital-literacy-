TASK 1 Create a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

I created my Digital Literacy infographic using Canva, as it provides a wide range of templates and design elements that are easy to use for beginners.
The infographic is titled “Digital Literacy: A Smart Student Guide” and focuses on three important aspects: understanding digital literacy, safe internet practices,
and email etiquette.
In the first section, I explained digital literacy as the ability to use digital tools effectively for communication, learning, and productivity.
The second section highlights important safety practices such as not sharing OTPs, using strong passwords, and avoiding suspicious links. The third section focuses on
email etiquette, including using proper subject lines, polite language, and professional sign-offs.
While creating the infographic, I found it interesting to explore how visual elements like icons and colors can make information more engaging and easier to understand.
However, one challenge I faced was selecting the most important points and keeping the design simple without overcrowding it with too much information.
